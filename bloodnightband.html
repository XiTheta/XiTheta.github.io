About Us

Bloodnight Suckmetal & The Real Devil >X3 is a band formed by the unlikely pairing of Bloodnight Suckmetal, a fierce and enigmatic lead vocalist and guitarist, and The Real Devil, a virtuosic bass player with a love for heavy metal music. The two met in a dark alleyway in the heart of Oklahoma City, where Bloodnight was performing a impromptu concert. The Real Devil, who was on his way to a gig, was immediately drawn to Bloodnight's raw talent and unique style. The two struck up a conversation and discovered that they shared a love for music, especially the heavy metal genre. They decided to form a band together and started performing in local warehouses and clubs. Their unique blend of blood-pumping, heavy metal music and Bloodnight's powerful and soul-stirring vocals quickly gained them a dedicated fan base and they have become one of the most popular bands in the heavy metal scene. With their music and live shows, Bloodnight Suckmetal & The Real Devil >X3 have carved a place for themselves in the hearts of music lovers everywhere.

Members

  • Guitar/Vocals: Bloodnight Suckmetal

    Bloodnight Suckmetal is the band's lead vocalist and guitarist. He has been playing guitar since the age of 15 and his love for metal music led him to create the band. Suckmetal is also known for his powerful and aggressive stage presence.

  • Bass: The Real Devil

    The Real Devil is the band's bassist and backing vocalist. He founded the band with Suckmetal and his style of playing the bass is considered to be one of the most unique in the metal scene.

  • Drums: Dante Alighieri

    Dante is the band's drummer. He joined the band in its early days and is considered one of the most talented drummers in the metal scene, his drumming style is characterized by his high energy and fast-paced beats
